About Me

Student and Aspiring Physicist

I am a student in my final year at Brock University, finishing a Bachelors of Science with a major in Physics and a minor in Mathematics. I am passionate about theories of gravity and causality within general relativity and quantum mechanics. I would like to use the skills and knowledge from this degree to push myself to explore these topics further, with a focus on theories of quantum gravity.
